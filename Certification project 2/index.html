The Amazing Life Of Nikola Tesla

The man who invented the 20th century

The astounding mad scientist life of Nikola Tesla. Just who was this pioneer of radio, radar, and wireless communication? We discover his legacy in the work of today’s scientists and artists. Samantha Hunt’s novel The Invention of Everything Else is a fictional portrait of Tesla. Monologist Mike Daisey tells us how Tesla X-rayed Mark Twain’s head. And across the country, garage inventors toil in obscurity at the next breakthrough that will change the world.

Nikola Tesla Timeline

A timeline of important events, achievements, setbacks and triumphs.

  • 1859 - Nikola Tesla is born.
  • 1870 - Brush with death , due to contracting cholera. Was hospoitalised for 1 year.
  • 1875 - Enrolls to a Polytechnic based college on a military scholarship.
  • 1880 - Moves to Prauge to Study philosophy.
  • 1881 - Begins his career as chief electrical engineer.
  • 1882 - Goes to work for the Edison.
  • 1885 - Submits some of his first patents.
  • 1887 - Re-imagines the electric motor.
  • 1890 - Begins his work on wireless power transmission.
  • 1891 - Patens the Tesla coil.
  • 1943 - Death.
